CaaS for Portugal

Introduction

In preparation of the on-site visit regarding the Containers as a Service (CaaS) offering made in Spain some this document contains high-level highlights of the kind of doubts and questions that the Portuguese team has at the moment; not having an intimate knowledge of the solution it is likely that not all of them are apropriate but they are still a reference that can help guide the information sharing process

Doubts

Management

  1. What are the components used for monitoring?

  2. Is the monitoring integrated (or integratable) into existins GSMA-based solutions or should it be considered a completely different approach (self-contained and not dependent or integrated into anything else, e.g. Prometheus/Graphana/ELK/etc)?

  3. Is the monitoring for the Containers different from the one used for the managed-from infrastructure?

  4. What is the backup approach for the containers? .

Additional services

  1. Is migration analysis a part of the offering?

  2. If migration analysis is part of the offering, is it standalone or articulated with other units (e.g. Cloud, GBS)

CaaS and Cloud offerings

  1. What is the relation with ICP? Can ICP be leveraged/proposed as a basis for the CaaS offering? .

Costing, princing and scope

  1. What are the key price drivers in the offering?

  2. Is there a responsability matrix or service description that quickly identifies the scope?

  3. Are there minimum reuquirements for an initial setup?

Non-functional requirements

  1. What is the overall DR approach?

  2. Is Business Continuitiy possible and/or integrated into the design?
